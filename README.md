# 🦜 Corsario del Mar

> *"Every can hides a secret. Every secret hides a place."*

---

## What We Are

**Corsario del Mar** ("Corsair of the Sea") is a premium watermelon-flavoured canned cocktail brand built on a single defensible premise: a drinks product can be a tourism activation engine.

We are not a drinks company that does marketing. We are a **discovery platform disguised as a drinks brand** — one that routes engaged, spending consumers toward the hidden communities, pirate histories, and buried ingredients of Spain's undiscovered coastline.

---

## The Product

A **330ml craft Tinto de verano** — Spanish red wine blended with natural watermelon soda, brewed in Murcia, Spain. Each batch incorporates one unique locally-sourced botanical from the mystery location featured in that month's box: Galician cliff rosemary, La Vera smoked pimentón, wild Padrón pepper extract, Cantabrian Atlantic sea salt.

Every ingredient is traceable. Every producer is named. Every can is tied to a real place.

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Format | 330ml aluminium can |
| ABV | 5% |
| Base | Monastrell or Tempranillo (sourced per batch from the mystery region) |
| Core flavour | Natural Almería watermelon essence |
| Hidden ingredient | One locally-sourced botanical per monthly batch — unique per location |
| Production | Co-packed in Murcia, Spain |
| Dietary | Vegan · Contains sulphites · No artificial additives |

---

## The Mascot

**Loro** — an illustrated monk parakeet styled as a corsair's first mate. He appears on every can, every box, and every piece of content. He narrates each QR experience in English. He knows exactly where the secret is buried. He will not tell you directly. That is the point.

---

## The Mechanic — Core Differentiator

Every can carries a **unique QR code** on its base. Scanning it (no app, mobile browser only) unlocks a five-screen mystery experience:

1. **The Video** — 60–90 seconds of cinematic footage of a real hidden Spanish location. No name. No caption. No GPS coordinates. Loro narrates.
2. **The Historical Fact** — one documented story from that location: a corsair raid, a sunken galleon record, a fishing cooperative that supplied the Spanish Armada. Sourced from regional archives.
3. **The Local Ingredient** — the specific botanical used in your can's batch. The producer's name. The farm it came from.
4. **The Photo Challenge** — one cryptic reference photograph taken at the exact location. Your mission: travel there, find the viewpoint, recreate the shot.
5. **The Submission** — upload your matching photograph. One verified submission = one prize draw entry.

---

## The Prize

**One winner. Every month. No exceptions.**

The subscriber who submits a verified photograph — recreated from the exact viewpoint of the monthly reference image — wins:

- ✈️ Return flights to Spain (from their nearest major airport)
- 🏨 3 nights accommodation near the mystery location
- 🗺️ A private guided visit to the exact site
- 🍉 3 months free subscription

**Solve all 4 mysteries in a month = 4 entries. Each verified photo = 1 entry.**

## The Investor Thesis

Spain received **85.1 million international tourists in 2023** (INE). Over 70% of overnight stays concentrated in five destinations. Meanwhile, thousands of coastal heritage communities — with documented corsair histories, active fishing cooperatives, and regionally unique ingredients — received almost no economic benefit from tourism.

We route high-value, experience-seeking consumers from Northern Europe directly toward these communities, before they book a flight, through a product they already consume every month.

**We are not selling drinks. We are selling the urge to disappear.**

---

## The Scale

Spain alone provides **500+ qualifying mystery locations** — enough for over a decade of unique content before any location repeats. At 4 cans per subscriber per month, 10,000 subscribers consume only 40 unique locations per month.

**Phase 1:** Spain (17 autonomous regions — Andalusia, Galicia, Murcia, Valencia, Cantabria)
**Phase 2:** Portugal · Italy
**Phase 3:** Greece · France · India (Goan Portuguese maritime legacy · Malabar coast)

Same model. Local ingredient. Local story. Local archive. Every time.

## The Founding Story

> *In Cádiz, where the Atlantic shoulders itself against the old city walls like something that has not forgotten a grievance, August arrives as a verdict — white light, white stone, the smell of salt and cut sandía drifting up from the harbour where old Fermín sat each midday with his knife and his patience... and as the parakeet screamed again, twice this time, which meant a boat was coming in from open water, Fermín looked up and saw, rounding the headland from the west, a vessel he did not recognise, flying no flag at all.*

This is the founding myth of Corsario del Mar. The hidden chart. The corsair routes. The place that exists but is never shown. The question the brand never stops asking: **can you find it?**
