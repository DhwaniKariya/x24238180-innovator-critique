The Secret

"The corsairs knew this coast."

Late-16th-century records describe repeated privateer attacks along the Galician coast, including the wider Muros-Noia area. Coastal promontories in this region were used as practical lookout points by local communities for generations.

Source: Consello da Cultura Galega, Maritime Archive

The Can

Not a mixer.
A message in a bottle.

Corsario del Mar is a premium craft Tinto de verano — Spanish red wine blended with natural watermelon soda, produced in partnership with craft beverage makers in Murcia. Spain is one of Europe's leading watermelon producers, and we currently prioritise sourcing from Andalusia and Murcia.

Each batch carries one additional locally-sourced botanical from that month's mystery location, revealed only when you scan the QR code. This is not a flavour gimmick. It is a traceable supply chain and a story that begins in a specific field, tended by a specific person, in a specific place that you are invited to find.

🍷

Every ingredient is traceable to a named producer in a real Spanish community. Their name and photograph appear in the QR experience linked to your can.
